# Rank and Rent Compliance Guidelines

## Core Principles

This site routes inquiries to our chosen fulfillment partner for 1031 exchange advisory and property identification support. All content must comply with rank and rent best practices.

## Content Rules

### Never Claim Past Projects or Specific Clients

- Frame scenarios as examples of the type of engagement we can handle
- Use generic descriptions of client situations
- Avoid specific project names, addresses, or client identifiers
- Invite contact to share real references upon request

### Example Framing

- Correct: "Example of the type of engagement we can handle"
- Incorrect: "We completed this exchange for Client X in 2023"

### No Unverifiable Claims

- Do not fabricate timelines or outcomes
- Do not claim specific results or performance metrics
- Do not make unverifiable statements about market conditions
- Stick to factual, educational content only

### No Testimonials or Reviews

- No testimonials from clients
- No review stars or ratings
- No badges or certifications without verifiable sources
- No client quotes or case study details

### Tax and Legal Disclaimers

- Educational content only
- Not tax, legal, or investment advice
- 1031 defers income tax on qualifying real property
- Does not remove transfer or documentary taxes
- Users should consult Qualified Intermediary and tax advisor before acting

### Securities Disclaimers

- DST or TIC may be securities
- We do not sell securities
- We provide introductions to licensed providers only
- Include securities disclaimer when DST or TIC is mentioned

## Disclosure Requirements

### Footer Disclosure

Include this disclosure on all pages:

"Disclosure: This site routes inquiries to our chosen fulfillment partner for 1031 exchange advisory and property identification support."

### Site Purpose Statement

This site helps investors identify potential replacement properties for Section 1031 exchanges. This site is not a Qualified Intermediary, law firm, broker, or CPA.

## Content Style

### Plain English

- Use short sentences
- Avoid contractions
- No em dashes
- Clear, factual language

### No Hype

- Avoid salesy tone
- Use direct, confident language
- Focus on facts and education
- Emphasize compliance and deadlines

## Verification Requirements

### Location Information

- DO NOT MAKE UP INFORMATION WITHOUT VERIFYING IT TO BE TRUE, ESPECIALLY FOR OUR LOCATIONS
- Research population, employers, and market data before writing
- Use accurate, verifiable information only

### Service Descriptions

- Describe services accurately
- Focus on what is included
- Avoid unverifiable claims about outcomes
- Frame as examples of engagement types

- [ ] No testimonials or reviews
- [ ] Tax and legal disclaimers included
- [ ] Securities disclaimers included when applicable
- [ ] Footer disclosure present
